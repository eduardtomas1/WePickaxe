Security

Security at WePickaxe

Our approach to protecting data and earning trust.

Security principles

  • On-device processing to minimize data exposure.
  • Least-privilege access for internal tools and infrastructure.
  • Continuous monitoring and rapid response workflows.

Data protection

WePickaxe products are designed to keep personal photos on-device. When data is stored in WePickaxe systems, it is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Vulnerability reporting

If you believe you have discovered a security issue, please contact us at security@wepickaxe.com. We aim to acknowledge reports within 72 hours.

Responsible disclosure

We welcome responsible disclosure and ask researchers to avoid accessing or modifying customer data. Please provide a clear description of the issue and steps to reproduce.

Questions

For security inquiries or partner audits, email security@wepickaxe.com.
