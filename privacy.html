Privacy policy

Last updated January 2024.

Overview

WePickaxe builds privacy-first photo intelligence. Our products are designed to keep sensitive photo data on-device whenever possible. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Data we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate our services:

  • Contact details you provide when you join a waitlist or request a demo.
  • Basic usage telemetry that helps us improve reliability and performance.
  • Support communications you send to our team.

On-device processing

For products like Keeply and the Library Health Kit, photo analysis runs on-device. We do not upload photo libraries or raw images to our servers as part of core processing.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide product updates.
  • To improve the quality and safety of our experiences.
  • To comply with legal obligations if required.

Third-party services

We use trusted service providers for email delivery and analytics. These providers are required to protect your data and only process it on our instructions.

Your choices

  • You can opt out of product updates at any time.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

Contact us

For privacy questions or requests, email privacy@wepickaxe.com.
