What is WePickaxe?

WePickaxe is an independent app studio dedicated to creating meaningful iOS applications. We're passionate developers who believe in building software that respects users and solves real problems. Every app we create is crafted with care, privacy-first principles, and attention to detail.

What apps do you make?

Our flagship product is Keeply, a smart photo cleanup app for iOS that helps you declutter your photo library by detecting duplicates, blurry shots, and low-quality images. We've created 8 apps total with over 47,500 downloads. We focus on utility apps that make your digital life easier while protecting your privacy.

When will Keeply be available?

Keeply is currently in development and coming soon to the iOS App Store. We're taking our time to ensure it meets our high standards for quality and user experience. Want to be notified when we launch? Sign up through our contact form and select "Keeply launch notification."

How do you handle privacy?

Privacy is at the core of everything we build. All processing in our apps happens 100% on-device ‚Äî your data never leaves your phone. No cloud uploads, no tracking, no data collection. We don't have access to your photos, and we never will. Your privacy isn't a feature; it's a fundamental right.

Are your apps free?

We offer both free and pro versions of our apps. The free version provides core functionality for everyone, while the pro version unlocks advanced features for power users and enthusiasts. No hidden fees, no subscriptions that auto-renew without warning ‚Äî just transparent, honest pricing.
